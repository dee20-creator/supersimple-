About Us

Nthawi Yathu Movement is a youth-led organisation based in Malawi that empowers young people in mental health, academic growth, and spirituality.At Nthawi Yathu Movement, we work with young people across Malawi to support mental well-being, promote academic excellence, and nurture spiritual growth. Our activities include peer-led mental health support sessions, school outreach campaigns, youth dialogue circles, academic mentorship, and safe spaces where youths can share their challenges and ideas. We aim to build a generation that is mentally strong, spiritually grounded, and academically driven. Our time. Our voice. Our change.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and empowering platform for youths to thrive mentally, spiritually, and academically.

Our Vision

A resilient generation of Malawian youths equipped to lead with mental clarity, purpose, and passion.
